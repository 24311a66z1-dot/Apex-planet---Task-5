APEXPLANET TASK 4

Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation

Validating business assumptions through statistical analysis and communicating findings using data storytelling techniques.

Project Overview

The objective of this project was to validate whether differences between product category profits were statistically significant. Using hypothesis testing, business assumptions were evaluated through quantitative analysis rather than intuition. This project demonstrates how statistics can support evidence-based decision making.

Tools & Technologies

Python

SciPy

Pandas

Statistics

Hypothesis Statement

Null Hypothesis (H₀)

There is no significant difference between Technology and Furniture profits.

Alternative Hypothesis (H₁)

There is a significant difference between Technology and Furniture profits.

Statistical Results

T Statistic

7.1232

P Value

1.246e-12

Decision

Reject H₀

Confidence Level

95%

Interpretation

Since the p-value is significantly lower than 0.05, the null hypothesis was rejected. The analysis confirms that Technology and Furniture categories have statistically significant differences in profitability.

Business Impact

Statistical validation improves confidence in business decisions. Organizations can prioritize profitable categories and allocate resources more effectively when decisions are backed by evidence. This project demonstrates the practical value of analytics in business strategy.
