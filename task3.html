APEXPLANET TASK 3

Interactive Dashboard Development

Designed and developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to transform raw business data into meaningful insights and support data-driven decision making.

Project Overview

The objective of this project was to create a professional and interactive business intelligence dashboard using Power BI. The dashboard enables users to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer trends and regional performance through interactive visualizations and KPI metrics. The project focused on making business data easier to understand, analyze and communicate.

Dashboard Features

Total Sales KPI

Displays overall sales performance across all categories.

Total Profit KPI

Tracks profitability and business growth.

Visualizes sales contribution from different regions.

Monthly Trends

Shows sales patterns and seasonal fluctuations.

Interactive Filters

Allows dynamic exploration of business metrics.

Key Business Insights

  • Technology category generated the highest profit.
  • West region showed strong sales performance.
  • Monthly sales trends revealed seasonal variations.
  • Interactive KPIs improved business monitoring.
  • Dashboard simplified complex business analysis.
  • Visual reporting enhanced decision-making efficiency.

Business Value Delivered

The Power BI dashboard transformed raw business data into a professional reporting solution. It allows stakeholders to quickly identify profitable categories, high-performing regions and important sales trends without manually analyzing spreadsheets. The dashboard improves reporting efficiency and supports evidence-based decision making.
